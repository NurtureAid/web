Simpler Care, Stronger Bonds

Our Story

NurtureAid helps us Millenials and GenZ trying to get ahead of this competitive world. Most of these times, we don't find the time to care for our loved ones, even though we dearly want to! Our busy lives, distance and nuclear family structures make caring for loved ones difficult in-person. So, we want to find people to care for them. But it's challenging to find the right people, monitor their performance remotely and remain properly involved in the caring process. And let's not forget about the mountains thousands of medical reports that pains us when we try to find a relevant report for our loved ones.

So, a team of 3 students from United International University with a knack of solving the worlds problems using state-of-the-art technology has come up with NurtureAid. It is a cross-platform mobile application that offers a 5-phased solution to taking care of your loved ones:

  • Personalized Care
  • Real-time Monitoring
  • Centralized Report Link Sharing
  • Family Circles
  • AI-IoT Powered Application

So come join us in our journey to Simpler Care, Stronger Bonds!
