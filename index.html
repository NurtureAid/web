Our Mission

NurtureAid aims to streamline and simplify the process of caring for dependent individuals by leveraging advanced technology such as AI and IoT. The ultimate goal is to provide comprehensive care while strengthening family bonds.

Our Plan

To customize care and recommend caregivers based on individual needs for a personalized experience. Integrated wearables with AI-IoT detect hazards like seizures, and track health. Secure cloud storage and link sharing to allow seamless storage and access to medical reports.

Our Vision

NurtureAid envisions a future where distance and other life responsibilities are not a barrier in caring for our beloved ones with the facility of caring remotely yet comprehensively.

Offer a more personalized care for your loved ones.

We offer a personalized experience so that the app does not feel overwhelming for people of all ages.

  • Personalized onboarding to tailor your experience for your needs.
  • A user dashboard that adapts to your features and activities.
  • Update, track, and personalize health logs on the go.

Monitor your loved ones in real-time!

Our 3-phased tracking makes it easy to checkup on what your loved ones are doing and how safe they are.

  • See what your loved ones are doing real-time.
  • Let the caregiver care for your loved one while you tracking the appointment.
  • Simultaneous SOS system for any kind of danger.
  • Danger and fall detection through cameras and wearables (freemium).

Store your docs centrally and easily share them!

Tried of stacking paper-based reports and documents on your desks? We got you covered!

  • A centralized report storage system dedicated for a specific user.
  • Easy sharing of reports using open links.
  • Anyone in your family circle can see and share your loved ones' reports.

Stay connected and updated using Family Circles

Need real-time and recent updates for everyone? Our family circle serivce is here to help!

  • Create an internal family circle to actively share all reports and activities.
  • Book appointments for your loved ones through anyone in the family circle.
  • Share reports of your loved one from anyone in the family circle.
  • Anyone in your circle can keep track of your loved ones' activities.
  • Simultaneous SOS alerts sent to everyone in the circle.

State-of-the-art tech to provide a better experience

We bring you state-of-the-art technologies to help aid your experience with us!

  • Recommender systems to recommend the best caretakers according to your needs.
  • Adaptive AI-assisted dashboard.
  • IoT-based AI fall detection system through wearables and cameras.
  • Chatbots to help get the most out of our app.
